About us Why buy with us

What makes us special!

Belle Pastry is a company that provides desserts as their main products. So it falls under the category of bakery services in the nature of business and the form of our business is partnerships. The reason why we chose dessert as our products is because we strongly believe that people tend to look for desserts as it is a must after having a full meal course. Aside from that, dessert can be eaten anytime and in any place. We offer a variety of desserts that are able and appealing to attract customers in terms of physical and taste of the dessert. Our cafes are a hybrid business as we not only provide desserts online, but we also provide delivery and cash on delivery to ensure customers of all ages and occupations are able to enjoy our delectable desserts. We also openly welcome our customers who prefer walking in through our cafes to enjoy freshly baked desserts straight from the oven.
