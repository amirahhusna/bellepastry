About Us

Belle Pastry is a company that provides desserts as their main products. So it falls under the category of bakery services in the nature of business. The reason why we chose dessert as our products is because we strongly believe that people tend to look for desserts as it is a must after having a full meal course. Aside from that, dessert can be eaten anytime and in any place.
