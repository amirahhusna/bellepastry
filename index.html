Unique style and design in their cafe. Not really much food to have it but can enjoy their coffee with their environment. Overall everything is nice.

Undeniably one of the most popular places in seapark for dessert. Never empty. Service is always good here, the staff are always friendly and polite.

hyejae.

Minnie.

Call in advance to make reservations because it was packed on a Saturday night! We ordered the their Korean Strawberry Cake & Bingsu, the juices had a clean finish & the smoothie was comfortably sweet. Ambience is a bit too noisy during a full house though.

Sung Ji.

Tried their recommended Bingsu - it’s great for people like me who love ice shave things. Ambience was also great - felt like being in a warm and cozy glasshouse with plants.
